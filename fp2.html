Goals

Educate. Empower. Achieve.

At the “I Have A Dream” Foundation, we provide individualized social, emotional, and academic support to young people from under-resourced communities from 1st grade all the way through college, along with guaranteed tuition support. We believe that when given equal access to the resources they need to succeed, all children can ignite their innate potential and achieve their dreams.

The Numbers

3x

Dreamers are over 3x more likely to earn a bachelor’s degree than their peers who experience income inequality.

91%

91% of Dreamers graduate High School, compared to 74% of students experiencing income inequality nationally.

$1M

College graduates earn, on average, an additional $1 Million in lifetime earnings.
